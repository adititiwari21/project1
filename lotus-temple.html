5. LOTUS TEMPLE

The Lotus Temple is a beautiful and unique temple located in Delhi, India. It is shaped like a lotus flower, with its white marble petals spread out to create a stunning visual effect. The temple was completed in 1986 and is a major attraction for both tourists and locals. Inside the Lotus Temple, there is a large, open hall where people come to meditate and reflect. The temple is known for its peaceful and serene environment, which makes it a special place for visitors seeking tranquility. It’s open to people of all faiths and backgrounds, emphasizing the principles of unity and harmony. The Lotus Temple is also notable for its architectural design. The lotus shape symbolizes purity and beauty, and the building is surrounded by lovely gardens and pools that enhance its overall charm. It’s recognized as one of the most iconic landmarks in Delhi and has received many awards for its design and construction.
