7. CHARMINAR

The Charminar is a famous landmark located in Hyderabad, India. Built in 1591, this historic structure is known for its four grand arches and towering minarets. The name "Charminar" means "Four Minarets" in Urdu, which describes the building's unique design. The Charminar is situated in the heart of Hyderabad’s old city and is surrounded by busy markets. The area around it is lively, with lots of shops selling everything from jewelry to street food. The building itself is made of granite and has intricate designs that reflect the architectural style of the time. Visitors often come to see the Charminar not just for its historical significance, but also to enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the surrounding streets. It’s a symbol of Hyderabad’s rich history and cultural heritage, and remains a popular spot for both tourists and locals.
